support

We enjoy the challenges that come with living on a boat. The power limitations and reduced connectivity has re-shaped how we work — in a good way. We now create our own software (see mission), an open-source ecosystem (see tools ecosystem) of creative tools. We document our experience and findings on this website, which doubles as a wiki.

Donations ensure that we can continue to dedicate our time to the creation of small, playful, open-source software & art projects(books, games etc), with a focus on documenting means of reusing and repairing older devices/programs, as well as archiving traditional seasteading skills.
